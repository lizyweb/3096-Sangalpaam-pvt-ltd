The Automatic Sanitizer Dispenser from Sangalpaam Pvt. Ltd is designed to promote hygiene and safety by providing a contactless solution for hand sanitization. Ideal for public spaces, offices, and homes, it ensures effective germ protection without the need for physical touch.

Benefits

Enhanced Hygiene

Reduces the spread of germs and bacteria through a touch-free operation.

Convenience

Automatically dispenses the right amount of sanitizer, minimizing waste.

Safety

Promotes regular hand sanitization, crucial in preventing infections.

Cost-Effective

Long-lasting and durable, reducing the need for frequent replacements.

Features

  1. Touch-Free Operation: Infrared sensor technology for automatic dispensing.
  2. Adjustable Dispensing Volume: Customize the amount of sanitizer dispensed per use.
  3. Durable Design: Made from high-quality materials to withstand frequent use.
  4. Battery Operated: Offers portability and ease of installation without the need for wiring.
  5. Refillable Reservoir: Easy-to-refill container, compatible with various liquid sanitizers.

Installation

  1. Simple Setup: Can be easily mounted on walls or placed on stands in any desired location.
  2. User-Friendly: Quick and hassle-free installation process with minimal maintenance required.
  3. Versatile Placement: Suitable for use in offices, schools, hospitals, malls, and homes.

Sangalpaam Pvt. Ltd ensures that the Automatic Sanitizer Dispenser is a reliable solution for maintaining a safe and hygienic environment.
